Interesting facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica
  • Cicadas use trees to get nutrients
  • Cicadas can sing as loud as chainsaws

Interesting facts about Camels

  • Camels have humps on their backs
  • Camels live in the desert
  • Camels can live for a long time without food and water

Interesting facts about Chameleons

  • There are over 200 species of chameleons
  • Half of all the chameleons live in Madagascar
  • Chameleons can see ultraviolet light

Interesting facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons can fly more than 6000 feet up in the air
  • The fastest speed a pigeon has ever flew was recorded to be 92.5 mph
  • It is estimated that there are about 400 million pigeons worldwide

Interesting facts about Pandas

  • Pandas like to eat bamboo even though it has little nutrients
  • Pandas spend 12 to 16 hours a day just eating
  • Pandas do not hibernate
